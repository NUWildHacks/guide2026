# Prepare for WildHacks

## Before You Arrive

<Steps>
### Join the WildHacks 2026 Discord server
 
The WildHacks 2026 Discord server has *all* important information before, during, and after the event. **We recommend downloading the Discord app on your phone.** You'll get notifications of events during WildHacks and important announcements.

<Callout type="info">
  [Join the Discord server](https://discord.gg/7zrVDzeWKH) for future announcements, fun events, and more!
</Callout>
 
### Log in to the WildHacks Dashboard
 
Make sure you can access the [WildHacks Dashboard](https://dashboard.wildhacks.net). Log in using the GitHub account you used when registering for WildHacks.

_Having trouble? Reach out to an organizer in [#ask-organizers](https://discord.com/channels/1340046559298982019/1342775711722901554) in Discord._

<Callout type="info">
  You will need access to your WildHacker barcode to check in prior to opening ceremony.
</Callout>

### Pack the Essentials

- 2025-2026 student ID
- Laptop and laptop charger
- Phone and phone charger
- Toiletries
- Sleeping bag, blankets, and pillows (optional)

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  We will be checking IDs when you check in. See more information about
  [checking in](/logistics/checking-in).
</Callout>

</Steps>
