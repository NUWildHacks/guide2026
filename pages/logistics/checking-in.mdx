## **How** to Check In

When you arrive at the Technological Institute, turn left and head over to the check-in desk. **Have your student ID AND your phone ready with your [WildHacker Code](https://dashboard.wildhacks.net/code) visible.** An organizer will scan your code and check your ID. You'll then get some cool WildHacks swag and will be ready to start hacking!
