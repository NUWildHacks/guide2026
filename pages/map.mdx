# WildHacks Event Map

## Event Spaces

WildHacks programming will take place across two buildings connected to each other by an indoor bridge.

[Learn about leaving and re-entering event spaces during the event.](/logistics/leaving-the-venue)

### Tech

Check-in, workshops, and opening/closing ceremony will take place in Tech. The building is accessible via the main entrance on the west side of the building, which faces Sheridan Road.

**Northwestern University Techological Institute**<br />2145 Sheridan Road<br />Evanston, IL 60208<br />[View on Apple Maps](https://maps.apple.com/?address=2145%20Sheridan%20Rd,%20Evanston,%20IL%2060208,%20United%20States&auid=9021466912054288827&ll=42.057769,-87.675877&lsp=9902&q=Technological%20Institute)<br />[View on Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/KvQNxa82V3LPenuh8)

### Mudd

The [WildHacks Help Desk](/help-desk) and [WildSnacks Station](/logistics/food-and-drinks) will be located on the second floor of Mudd.

The area will be open 24/7 and will include [overnight spaces](/logistics/sleeping-and-showering).

Mudd Library**<br />2233 Tech Drive<br />Evanston, IL 60208<br />[View on Apple Maps](https://maps.apple.com/?address=2233%20Tech%20Dr,%20Evanston,%20IL%20%2060208,%20United%20States&auid=875104193315716512&ll=42.058282,-87.674389&lsp=9902&q=Mudd%20Library)<br />[View on Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/oxMTM9ysAxL3zPUW7)
